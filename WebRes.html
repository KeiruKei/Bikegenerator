Power to the Pedal:
Ride the Energy Wave with Bike-Powered Generator

It attempts to improve cycling overall by offering a dependable and portable way to charge different gadgets or accessories while out and about.

It achieves this by using some existing technology and mixing them together to create another technology with features that are able to produce electricity just by cycling.
