Power to the Pedal:
Ride the Energy Wave with Bike-Powered Generator

It attempts to improve cycling overall by offering a dependable and portable way to charge different gadgets or accessories while out and about.

It achieves this by using some existing technology and mixing them together to create another technology with features that are able to produce electricity just by cycling.

In an era where the bicycle has become an important source of sustainable and accessible transportation, a group of researchers has turned their attention towards a common challenge faced by cyclists: keeping their mobile devices powered on the go. The idea is straightforward: integrating generators into bicycles to offer a reliable energy source. Bicycles have gained popularity for their affordability, health benefits, flexibility, and convenience. However, with modern lifestyles relying heavily on mobile devices for connectivity and productivity, the concept of a bike-powered generator is more than a novelty; it's a potentially transformative solution for cyclists.
